ABOUT US

Welcome to Shawarma Oasis, a culinary gem born from the rich tapestry of flavors found in the Gulf countries. Established in the bustling streets of Dubai in the '90s, Shawarma Oasis has been a beloved destination for those seeking the perfect blend of fresh, authentic, and tantalizingly tasty shawarmas.

Now, we're thrilled to announce our arrival in Kerala, India, bringing the warmth and spice of the Gulf to this vibrant region. At Shawarma Oasis, we're dedicated to preserving the essence of Gulf cuisine while infusing it with local Kerala influences.

Under the passionate ownership of Mr. Ahmed Al-Mansoori, Shawarma Oasis continues to uphold its commitment to excellence in every bite. Our shawarmas are meticulously crafted using the freshest ingredients, ensuring each bite bursts with flavor and authenticity.

Join us at Shawarma Oasis and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the harmony of Gulf and Kerala flavors. Indulge in our mouthwatering shawarmas and experience the magic of fresh, authentic, and irresistibly tasty cuisine.
